Course Description

This interactive e-learning course will introduce you to User Experience (UX) design,the art of creating
products and services that are intuitive, enjoyable, and user-friendly. Gain a solid foundation in UX priciples
and learn to apply them in real-world scenarious through engaging modules and interactive exercises.
